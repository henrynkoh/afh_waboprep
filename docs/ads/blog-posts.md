**Body:**

**Introduction**  
If you’re turning a single-family home in Lewis, Thurston, or Pierce County into an Adult Family Home (AFH), you need to pass a local Building Official inspection before DSHS licensing. That means having the right data, drawings, and paperwork—without guesswork.

**What you need**  
- Property data (rooms, doors, windows, stairs, site) in one place  
- A floor plan that meets WABO/DSHS 15-604 (sleeping rooms A–F, egress, scale)  
- A clear path from “as-is” to “to-be” 6-bed AFH  
- A permit packet that matches your city (e.g. Centralia) checklist  

**How AFH WABO Prep by DIY helps**  
AFH WABO Prep by DIY is a free, open app that lets you:

1. **Entry Mode** — Enter case meta, rooms, doors, windows, stairs, and site. Export CSV/JSON for your own workflow or AI tools.  
2. **2D/3D Drawing** — Generate a 2D floor plan and 3D isometric from that data. Export SVG for your permit packet.  
3. **Pipeline** — Use built-in AI-agent prompts (Compliance, Design, Permit Ops, Red-Team) with Moltbot/Clawdbot or any AI runner to draft reports and packet contents.  
4. **Centralia Packet** — See the required submittal list and document names for City of Centralia and Lewis County.  
5. **Curriculum & Q&A** — Follow an 8-week consultant outline and Top 100 Q&A for WAC, DSHS 15-604, and best practices.  

**Case study: 2606 Cooks Hill Rd, Centralia**  
The app includes a case study for 2606 Cooks Hill Rd, Centralia 98531. You can use the same flow for any address: fill Entry Mode → generate 2D/3D → run the pipeline → assemble the packet.
